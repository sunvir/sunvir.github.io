Group Classes

Private Classes

Private art lessons are geared towards artist of all levels providing them with a foundation for creative expression in different mediums.

For private lessons, please contact me for more information and availability.

Camps

Art sessions and camps are fun and creative themed lessons. Kids create masterpieces using real artists' materials including oil, acrylic, watercolor paints, and more.

Camps are usually held in summer at the recreation centers where I usually hold my group classes. A limited number of camps are offered at my home studio as well. Please contact mefor information and availability.
